Eligibility for the LaRosa's Male and Female MVP of the Year

Eligibility for the "LaRosa's MVP of the Year" was open to all male and female high school athletes in the Greater Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky area for the 2004-2005 school year.

The athletes could be from any class. Beginning with the Class of 2003, an athlete can repeat as MVP of the Year award. The winners are judged solely on what they accomplished during the 2004-2005 school year.

This year's 12 athletes were nominated by their respective high schools, LaRosa's Committee members and the media. The "Female and Male MVP of the Year" will be announced at today's banquet.
