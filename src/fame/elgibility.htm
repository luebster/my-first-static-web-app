BUDDY LAROSA’S SPORTS HALL OF FAME
- Elgibility Requirements

A maximum of 5 athletes are inducted into the Hall of Fame
each year as follows:

  • One athlete who graduated prior to 1964
  • Up to four athletes who graduated from 1965 to ten years prior the current year
  • At least one female athlete
  • One coach who may still be active (Memorial Award)
